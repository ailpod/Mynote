2. 首先配置Jenkins产品渠道配置

![img](file:///C:\Users\39691\AppData\Local\Temp\ksohtml15268\wps2.jpg) 

 

(1) 这里配置子仓库地址 [http://192.168.1.250/gamepackage/(具体游戏分支).git](http://192.168.1.250/gamepackage/.git)

(3) 架构选择arm64-v8a (64位手机), 若是32位选择 arm64-v7a

(4) 最后配置ConfigVigame ,

Oppo/vivo：["Permissions:READ_PHONE_STATE","IsBC:true","HealthEnable:true","ADTest:false","BlackFirst:false","isShowPrivacy:True","extrawinTimer:40","isShowMore:True","Email:[yxhy@jiahewlkj.com](mailto:yxhy@jiahewlkj.com)"]
